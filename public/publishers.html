You’ve tried everything to get paid for good content. Now there is an easier way.

In the good old days, publishers created content and ads paid the way. But Internet ads are quickly becoming yesterday’s news. Ad blocking has grown significantly over the last few years. Now nearly 500 million people use software to stop ads.

Without some change, smaller publishers could disappear and larger publishers' staff will continue shrinking.


Enter Brave Payments.

Today, your visitors want minimal ads but you want maximum return for your great content. Brave gives both sides what they want.

How does it work? Readers may choose a monthly contribution amount which is divided among the publisher sites they visit most. And as Brave grows, so does your revenue.

Here is the best part. Neither the readers nor the publisher need to do anything different. They just keep surfing and you just keep publishing.

Well, one small detail...you do need to collect your cash.


Claim your publisher contributions.

The Brave browser automatically keeps track of the publisher sites each user visits. The more times that a user visits a site, the larger the proportion of the user’s monthly contribution is “ear-marked” for that publisher. These funds are held in escrow, growing as new micropayments are added. When contributions for a publisher exceed $100.00 USD, an email is sent to both the webmaster of the site and the registered domain owner. The email explains how to verify the ownership of your website with Brave Software.


Brave Software uses a sophisticated process, a lot like verifying a domain name, to verify publisher wallets. Just follow these steps:


  • Step 1: Open the email invitation sent from Brave Software to your webmaster. The email is titled "Brave Publisher Invitation" and includes all of the information needed to register.

  • Step 2: The email will direct you to a web page where you will need to confirm your site ownership and wallet ownership. Brave will transfer your payments on a continual basis to your wallet. If you don't already have a bitcoin wallet, it's easy to create one with either of these wallet providers: Bitgo and Coinbase.

  • Step 3: The web page will generate a unique text file for you to upload to your server. This ensures a secure handshake between Brave Software and you - preventing someone else from receiving your payments.

  • Final Step: Check your wallet balance frequently, and transfer funds to wherever you choose. It's your money. You earned it!

Skip the notification process and get registered today.

There is no need to wait for your site contribution to exceed 100.00 USD. You can sign up now so that your site is primed and ready for Brave user contributions.
